About our dog grooming service

Here at Barking Bubbles, you and your dog will always be greeted with a smile! Your dog will feel calm and comfortable while being surrounded with our salon scents of lavender and vanilla, followed by a warm, sudsy oxygen-infused bath and high-velocity hand dry and fluff.


Our proven technique provides your dog with a unique grooming experience. Your furry friend will no longer be hesitant or fearful of grooming once they become a client of Barking Bubbles Grooming & Boutique, where we truly believe in stress-free grooming!


We serve dogs and puppies in Jamestown, NY; Lakewood, NY; Chautauqua, NY; and the surrounding areas. Stop in today or call for an appointment! We also specialize in special needs dogs, so call us for more information.

Dog Groomer Service in Lakewood, NY

Pricing

The following prices are a guideline only. Pricing is based on the size, temperament and coat condition of your companion. If your companion is matted, you will be contacted to assist in determining whether a smooth trim would be the healthiest option for your pet. If we feel we can work with the matting, and you choose this option an additional fee may apply.

Your grooming package includes:

  • ~ Warm, Sudsy, Oxygen-Infused Bath ~
  • ~ High-Velocity Coat Dry ~
  • ~ Brush/Comb Out ~
  • ~ Trim/Hand Scissoring ~
  • ~ Nail Dremeling ~
  • ~ Ear Cleaning ~
  • ~ Anal Gland Expression (If Needed) ~

Choosing the right grooming salon for your puppy is extremely important!

  • ~ To alleviate future fears, introduce your puppy to grooming as soon as possible. ~
  • ~ Hold and play with your puppy’s paws frequently. Doing so will help them with the grooming process. ~
